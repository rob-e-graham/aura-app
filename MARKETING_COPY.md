For *how* AURA speaks (tone, principles, forbidden moves), see `AURA_VOICE.md`.

## 🔒 The locked tagline

> **AURA**
> *Unlock AI with timeless wisdom & self reflection*

Status: **canonical** as of Apr 27, 2026. Use this everywhere unless one of
the sanctioned variations below is a better fit for the medium.

### Why this tagline works

- **"Unlock AI"** — positions AURA at the front of the AI wave, but as a
  *guide* rather than another chatbot. AI is the door; AURA is the key.
- **"timeless wisdom"** — covers the 78-card archetypal foundation
  (the tarot lineage reframed) without saying "tarot" (which carries
  baggage, App-Store-review risk, and a narrow demographic signal).
- **"self reflection"** — names the actual user activity. Not predictions.
  Not fortune. The honest verb.
- **"& "** — the ampersand keeps the line short and elegant; reads better
  than "and" in serif type.

## Brand framework: the "Three doors"

Use this when explaining AURA in any longer format (press kit, About
page, deck slide, founder podcast pitch).

> **AURA opens three doors at once: AI, ancient wisdom, and your own
> reflection. The conversation between them is your reading.**

- **The AI door.** Premium and Pro tiers run a calm, considered LLM that
  responds to *you* — your name, your card, your context — without
  predicting, manifesting, or selling you on outcomes.
- **The wisdom door.** 78 archetypal cards reimagined with one-word
  modern names — Leap, Bloom, Stillness, Threshold — built from the
  symbolic structure of traditional tarot but stripped of the
  occult-shop aesthetic.
- **The reflection door.** Every reading ends with a question, not an
  answer. The user does the work; AURA holds the space.

> AURA is a calm AI guide for self-discovery — built around the wisdom of
> a 78-card archetypal deck and the patience of a good listener.
>
> Pull a card. Sit with the colour. Read the message. Talk to AURA — your

> its own chromotherapy palette, an archetypal name, and a quiet
> affirmation. Three-card spreads (Past · Present · Future), single-card

> What makes AURA different is what it isn't. It isn't fortune-telling.
> It isn't a chatbot trying to keep you scrolling. It isn't a wellness

> AI grounded in timeless wisdom, designed for stillness, not stimulation.
>
> AURA learns your name, your intention, your zodiac (if you want it to)
> and reflects what you bring back to you with care. Free tier includes
> full card readings and a generous AI quota. Premium adds unlimited
> conversation and memory across sessions. Pro adds the option to run

## Words & phrases the tagline unlocks (use freely)

Now that "AI" and "timeless wisdom" are explicitly in the tagline, these
are *available* in marketing copy where they read naturally:

- **timeless wisdom** — the canonical phrase for the 78-card knowledge base
- **calmer AI** — positions AURA against the noise of chatbot-of-the-week
- **archetypal** — instead of "tarot" or "spiritual"
- **a daily ritual** — for retention/habit messaging
- **grounded** — "AI grounded in wisdom" is a strong recurring phrase
- **unlock** — only in the *AI/wisdom* sense, never the wellness-cliché
  "unlock your potential" (still forbidden — see `AURA_VOICE.md`)

## Words & phrases still off-limits

The voice doc still applies. The tagline doesn't open the door to:
- "manifest", "manifesting"
- "fate", "destiny", "fortune", "predict"
- "psychic", "supernatural", "magic", "spell"
- "ancient wisdom" (we deliberately use "timeless wisdom" — sounds
  modern, not occult-shop)
- "high vibe / low vibe / raise your frequency"
- "transform your life", "level up", "unlock your potential"
- "sacred feminine", "divine masculine"

If the tagline tempts you to write "unlock your destiny" — stop.

## Visual pairing notes (for designers + Fam)

When the tagline appears with the AURA wordmark + sparkle:

- **Wordmark above, tagline below**, never inline.
- **Tagline in Cormorant Garamond Italic**, weight 300, 22% of the
  wordmark size. (E.g. wordmark 96pt → tagline 21pt.)
- **Always lowercase on social** ("unlock AI with timeless wisdom &
  self reflection").
- **Sentence case in formal contexts** ("Unlock AI with timeless wisdom
  & self reflection") — App Store, website, press.
- **Never all-caps** the tagline — only AURA itself is all-caps.
- **Letter spacing** on tagline: 0.04–0.06em (looser than body, tighter
  than the wordmark's 0.18em).
