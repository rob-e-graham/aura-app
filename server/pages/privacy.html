AURA — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Overview

AURA ("we", "our", "the App") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

2. Data We Collect

Account Data:

  • Email address (used for sign-in via one-time password)
  • User ID (generated automatically)

Profile & Personalisation Data:

  • Birth date, zodiac sign, and time of birth (if provided)
  • Name or preferred name (if provided)
  • Personalisation preferences (themes, moods, focus areas)

Usage Data:

  • AI token usage and subscription tier
  • Chat messages sent to the AI (processed to generate responses)
  • Memory data (recurring themes and patterns from your conversations)

Data We Do NOT Collect:

  • Location data
  • Contacts or phone book data
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access
  • Device identifiers for advertising
  • Browsing history outside the App

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Authentication: Your email is used solely for sign-in via OTP codes.
  • Personalisation: Birth date and preferences are used to generate personalised readings, horoscopes, and AI responses.
  • AI Responses: Chat messages are sent to our AI provider (Together.ai / DeepSeek) to generate responses. Messages are not stored by the AI provider beyond the immediate request.
  • Memory Sync: Your personalisation memory is synced to our database (Supabase) so it persists across devices.
  • Usage Tracking: Token usage is tracked to manage your subscription allowance.

4. Data Storage

  • Local storage: Preferences, readings, and chat history are stored on your device.
  • Cloud storage: Account data, subscription status, token balance, and memory data are stored in Supabase (hosted on AWS, encrypted at rest).
  • AI processing: Chat messages are processed by Together.ai's API. Messages are transmitted securely via HTTPS and are not retained by the AI provider after generating a response.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Authentication and database hosting — supabase.com/privacy
  • Together.ai: AI model inference — together.ai/privacy
  • RevenueCat: Subscription and purchase management — revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Apple App Store: Payment processing for subscriptions and in-app purchases

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data. Data is only shared with the third-party services listed above, strictly for the purpose of providing the App's functionality.

7. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained while your account is active.
  • Upon account deletion, all personal data is removed from our servers within 30 days.
  • Local data on your device is cleared when you delete the App.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data through the App's settings
  • Delete your account and all associated data via Settings > Account > Delete Account
  • Export your data by contacting us
  • Opt out of personalisation by using guest mode

9. Children's Privacy

AURA is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

10. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure authentication tokens, and encrypted database storage. However, no system is 100% secure.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the App.

12. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us at hello@auraguide.com
