AURA / Logo exploration

First-pass logo directions

Designed to feel modern, minimal, mystical but grounded. The aim here is not wellness cliché. It is quiet authority, emotional depth, and enough distinction to work across app, apparel, packaging, and campaign graphics.

AURA

Direction 01 — Quiet luxury wordmark

Minimal serif wordmark with wide tracking. This is the cleanest and most flexible direction. Feels premium, editorial, and calm without trying too hard.

Best for fashion / app / packaging
AURA

Direction 02 — Orb mark + wordmark

Ties the logo system directly to the chromatic orb language. Good if you want a more ownable mark for socials, favicon, app icon systems, and merch tags.

Best for brand system / icon use
AURA

Direction 03 — Ritual-modern signature

Softer, slightly more uncanny, and more spiritually coded. Strong for campaign use and editorial treatments, though a little less universal than Direction 01.

Best for campaigns / hero treatments
My recommendation: start from Direction 01 as the master logo, then use Direction 02 as the supporting brand mark. That gives AURA a clean core identity and an ownable symbol system.
